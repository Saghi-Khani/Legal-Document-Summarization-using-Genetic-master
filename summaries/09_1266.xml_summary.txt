Upon the hearing of the winding up application being called on before the District Registrar, the company made application for the adjournment of the winding up application. Deputy Commissioner of Taxation v Bedroff Pty Ltd ACN 079 158 955 [2009] FCA 1266 (30 October 2009) Mr Hayes opines that a voluntary administration with a deed of company arrangement would be the best outcome for the company. His opinion is that it would allow for the orderly realisation of assets in order to satisfy secured and unsecured creditors.
