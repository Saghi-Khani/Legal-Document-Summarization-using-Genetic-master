The applicant submitted that, because the Minister had been advised through the issues paper that the applicant had "not made any claims that require[d] assessment in relation to international obligations", he had been invited to make a decision without regard to the implicit non-refoulement obligation of the ICCPR. Direction 21 concerns "visa refusal and cancellation under section 501 of the Migration Act 1958 ". The Full Court's judgment underpinned part of the case originally advanced by the applicant. The Department's records showed he left and returned to Australia on a Yugoslavian passport.
