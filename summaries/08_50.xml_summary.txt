On 31 October 2006 the AAT again affirmed the decision that Mr Hill was not entitled to an invalidity service pension under the Act: Hill v Repatriation Commission [2006] AATA 925. It concluded, in terms of s 120(3), that the hypothesis that Mr Hill was suffering from the psychiatric disorder of alcohol intoxication in Vietnam before the clinical onset of alcohol dependence was a reasonable one. Clauses 4 and 5 of the AD SOP provide:   Factors that must be related to service   4. It proceeded accordingly. That reliably reflects his evidence. I so order. They are referred to below.
