The Applicants raise for consideration the following, consequential points of law in the statement of claim: (a) any grant of a petroleum lease to the First and Third Respondents in respect of any of the Claimed Land covered by ATP 259P would not be a pre-existing rights based act within the meaning of Subdiv I of Div 3 of Pt 2 of the NTA; and (b) they, as registered native title claimants, would have the right to negotiate pursuant to Subdiv P of Div 3 of Pt 2 of the NTA in respect of any proposed grant.
