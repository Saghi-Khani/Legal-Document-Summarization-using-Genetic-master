It is for that reason that s 69A(1) of the AAT Act includes a reference to any other Act, which was intended to refer to, among others, the power to award costs under s 67(13) of the SRC Act. Solicitors provide legal services which are taxable supplies. The Tribunal however, affirmed the 2002 decision. The GST payable is one eleventh of the price. Here, the Tribunal had no power to make an order that Telstra pay Ms Keen's costs of the 2002 proceedings.
