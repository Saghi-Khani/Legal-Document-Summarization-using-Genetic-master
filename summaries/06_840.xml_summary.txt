3 The second issue is whether the applicant's employment contributed to his reactive arthritis. The applicant therefore sought an order in this Court overturning that part of the Tribunal's decision which affirmed the delegate's finding with respect to that injury. The applicant appeals that decision. This is not to say that any activity undertaken at barracks which caused an injury would necessarily have been contributed to by employment. Accordingly, I consider that the concept of ' defence service ' as contained in the Veterans' Entitlements Act is analogous to the concept of ' employment ' contained in the 1971 Act.
