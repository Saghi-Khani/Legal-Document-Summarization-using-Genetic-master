Federal Agent Capaldo said the privilege notice was attached to the copy of the Birchgrove warrant when it was provided to Mr Oke, and that the Birchgrove warrant had been taken to pieces by Mr Oke. But whether the laptop computer contains only legal privileged documents, or any legal privileged documents, is not yet resolved. Ultimately, the laptop computer was removed from the premises by the AFP without having been opened and without any of its files having been inspected by the executing officer. In that event, they could not be the subject of a search warrant under the Act.
