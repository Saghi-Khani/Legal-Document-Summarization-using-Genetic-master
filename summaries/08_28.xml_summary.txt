The improper purpose allegation  27 On 13 November 2007, the CFMEU in-house solicitor, Mr Chris Gamble, wrote to Mr Hadgkiss with reference to the notice dated 29 October 2007 served on Mr Washington. The investigation was said to concern suspected contraventions of s 816 of the WR Act by Mr Setka and Mr McLoughlin at four named construction sites. It could not constitute a breach of s816.
