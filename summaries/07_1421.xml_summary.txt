The plaintiffs sought leave to join the insurer as an additional defendant seeking a declaration that the insurer was obliged to indemnify the directors in respect of any judgment in favour of the plaintiff companies arising out of any wrongful act within the terms of the policy. KMF notified a claim and the Insurers denied liability. The Court also rejected the proposed joinder on discretionary considerations. The first relates to defamation (cl 1.2.1). That is a matter which should be capable of determination largely upon the pleadings, agreed facts and documents. Clause 3.3 is relevant.
