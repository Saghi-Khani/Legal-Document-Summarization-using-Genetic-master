The issue before the Court is whether products manufactured and sold by the applicant are exempt from goods and services tax ('GST') because they are beverages of a kind that fall within Items 11 or 12 of the table ('the Table') in cl 1 of Sch 2 to the GST Act, respectively non-alcoholic carbonated beverages consisting wholly of juices of fruits or vegetables or, non-alcoholic non-carbonated beverages consisting of at least 90% by volume of juices of fruits or vegetables.
