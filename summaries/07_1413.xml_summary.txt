Section 425(1) requires the Tribunal to invite an applicant to appear before a Tribunal to give evidence and present arguments relating to "the issues arising in relation to the decision under review". The Tribunal had held a hearing. The boy and his mother arrived in Australia on 8 May 1997. The Federal Magistrate did not err in so doing. The Tribunal affirmed the decision of the delegate to refuse to grant the appellants protection visas.
