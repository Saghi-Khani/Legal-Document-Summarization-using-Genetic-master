In particular, the applicant claims that as a result of the harassment, coercion and intimidation of the applicant's business and personal finances engaged in by the respondents in breach of section 60 Trade Practices Act , he suffered personal injury  42 In my view the applicant lacks standing in respect of this claim. ORDER   1. 106 No cause of action is disclosed by this Count. Claims with respect to financial damage have clearly vested in the applicant's trustee in bankruptcy. Sections 51AA and 52 Trade Practices Act apply to conduct of corporations. The whole pleading must be struck out.
