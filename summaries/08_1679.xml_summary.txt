The Federal Magistrates Court dismissed the appeal on 22 July 2008. 8 The appellant's second claim that the primary judge failed to consider the fact that in his written submissions the Tribunal ignored his evidence also cannot be sustained. The appellant has not identified any particular procedural error said to have been made by the primary judge. The Tribunal deferred the hearing until the appellant could attend. The appellant has not appeared. He applied for a protection visa on 10 April 2007. The primary judge was not the original decision-maker. The primary judge refused this application.
