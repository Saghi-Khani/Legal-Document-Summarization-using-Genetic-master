The effect on competition which is proscribed by ss 45(2) and 45A (1) must be on a market in Australia: see ss 45(3) and 4E of the Act. The Statement of Claim pleads three markets for the supply by international airlines and acquisition by shippers of international air freight services, each of which is said to be a market in Australia. The 68 instances listed in the second [438] include the May 2003 Indonesia understanding at [438.17] and the UAE 2004 Level 5 Surcharge Understanding at [438.41]. Section 45A(1) is a deeming provision.
