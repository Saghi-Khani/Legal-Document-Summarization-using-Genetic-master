The Commissioner contends that, under both the Charge Card Facility and the Credit Card Facility, the card holder is afforded credit, because the card holder is not required to make immediate payment for the goods supplied or the services provided to the cardholder by the merchant. The Commissioner contends that the Fee Payments are made in connection with a supply of an interest in a debt, a credit arrangement or a right to credit and are therefore consideration for a financial supply.
