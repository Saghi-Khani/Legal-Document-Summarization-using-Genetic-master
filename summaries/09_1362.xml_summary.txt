Earlier this year I made orders providing for substituted service in respect of Mr Davies. Combis as liquidator of Seminars International Pty Ltd v Commissioner of Taxation [2009] FCA 1362 (13 November 2009) On 18 June this year, the liquidator sought an order against the Commissioner pursuant to s 588FF of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) (Corporations Act) for the payment of the sum of $125,471.40. That interest is $5956. Mr Davies has not appeared today. The amount of indemnity to which the Commissioner is entitled is $68,544.94 plus $3255.50.
