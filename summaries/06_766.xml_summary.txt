His Honour exercised the discretion to refuse relief for unwarrantable delay, without considering whether the Tribunal's decision was attended by jurisdictional error. The Tribunal's decision also determined whether the appellant son should be granted a protection visa. It was therefore appropriate that the application be dismissed. The following day, the appellant applied to the Federal Magistrates Court for judicial review of the Tribunal's decision.
