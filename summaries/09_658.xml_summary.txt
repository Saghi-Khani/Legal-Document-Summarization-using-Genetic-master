The first question --- s 9-15(3)(c) exemption The question raised by the declaration sought in paragraph 1(a) of the application can be expressed as follows:  Is the payment made by the Commonwealth to the applicant by way of reimbursement under the Scheme "a payment made by a government related entity to another government related entity ... specifically covered by an appropriation under an Australian law" within the meaning of s 9-15(3)(c) of the GST Act? Here there is a definite supply to the eligible passenger (Mr Egan).
