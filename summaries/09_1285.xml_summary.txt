The Federal Magistrate inferred that the Tribunal had considered the information provided by Mr Sharma as to whether the incident occurred because it "sought to verify" that information (at [9]) by its further inquiry to DFAT. DFAT ascertained that the article had been published by a local newspaper on 1 March 2005. The Tribunal's reasons then refer to the heading "Corroborative evidence".
