21 The respondents submitted that the notice of motion was not unreasonable. 2 On 31 May 2006, the applicant and the respondents filed written submissions as to costs. Pre-reform subsection 347(1) provides that a party to proceedings under the WR Act shall not be ordered to pay the costs of another party unless the first party instituted the proceedings vexatiously or without reasonable cause. As with s 824(1), the references in subs (2) to a 'proceeding' encompass a step in the proceeding, such as the institution and hearing of an interlocutory application.
