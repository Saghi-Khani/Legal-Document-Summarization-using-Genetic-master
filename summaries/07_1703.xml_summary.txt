That subsection provided in effect that an order could not be made under s 87 unless the Court was satisfied that an order in, or consistent with, the terms of the agreement reached between the parties could not be made under s 87A. This is an application for a consent determination of native title rights and interests held by the Ngurrara people in respect of land and waters within the Kimberley region of Western Australia that has been designated "Determination Area A". That is not the case here. Kogolo v State of Western Australia [2007] FCA 1703 (9 November 2007)
