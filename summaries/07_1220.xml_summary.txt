On the last day of the conference, Mr Messenger convened a meeting of the celebrants who had attended the conference to discuss, relevantly, the "standard fee" charged by celebrants conducting civil funeral ceremonies through funeral homes. The penalty suggested for Mr Messenger for attempting to induce contraventions of the Competition Code is $10,000 plus costs fixed in the amount $3,000, also payable in four instalments between December 2007 and June 2009. The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission and the respondents have reached agreement on the appropriate orders. These ceremonies are arranged and conducted by celebrants.
