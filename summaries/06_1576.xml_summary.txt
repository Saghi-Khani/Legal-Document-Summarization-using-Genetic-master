On 2 November 2004 the Tribunal received a change of address form from the appellant. 5 On 18 January 2005 the appellant filed an application seeking judicial review of the Tribunal's decision in the Federal Magistrates Court. On 4 November 2004 the Tribunal sent a further letter to the appellant at her new address advising of a new hearing date set for 26 November 2004. The appeal should be dismissed with costs fixed at $900. The appellant did not specifically claim that she left China because of being persecuted for Falun Gong activities. She did not attend the hearing.
