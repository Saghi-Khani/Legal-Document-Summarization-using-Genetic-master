In Re LOCM Pty Ltd (Administrators Appointed) (1997) 79 FCR 35 , Goldberg J came to the view that the court has power under section 447A to make an order extending the time fixed by section 439B(2). Mr Chamberlain informed the creditors that a further extension would involve the necessity for an application to the court, and the creditors resolved that Mr Chamberlain should make that application for an extension of the adjournment period to 1 June 2009. The adjournment which is now sought is 40 business days from 20 April 2009.
