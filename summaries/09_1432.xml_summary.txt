Mr Byrnes then turned his attention to the holders of units, writing to them on 13 March 2009, seeking proxies for the proposed meeting of 9 April 2009. The letter said that Mr Byrnes would provide no undertaking to pay any costs and that the costs to date was a matter for the Court. Counsel for the Macquarie Companies then announced that the Macquarie Companies would seek an order for costs on an indemnity basis. On 2 March 2009, BrisConnections issued a call notice to members of the Trusts for the payment of the second instalment by 29 April 2009.
