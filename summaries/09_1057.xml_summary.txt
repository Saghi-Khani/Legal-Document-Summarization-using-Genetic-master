That subsection provides as follows:   A party to a proceeding before the Tribunal may appeal to the Federal Court of Australia, on a question of law, from any decision of the Tribunal in that proceeding. Generally speaking, a finding of fact is not reviewable by the court. Section 43(2B) merely requires that there be a " reference " to the evidence.
