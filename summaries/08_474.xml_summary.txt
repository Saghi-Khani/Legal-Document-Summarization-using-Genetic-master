Section 468(1) is in well known terms and renders, relevantly here, any transfer of shares or alteration in the status of the members of Rothwells, made after the commencement of the winding up of that company by the Court, void, unless the Court otherwise orders. Mr Denoon instructed his accountants to document an agreement between CPL and CCPL for the sale of all of the Rothwells shares for $1.00. Carringbush Corporation Pty Ltd v ASIC [2008] FCA 474 (10 April 2008) Thus, no prejudice to creditors arose. Mr Clark would invest $1.8 million in the Carringbush Trust.
