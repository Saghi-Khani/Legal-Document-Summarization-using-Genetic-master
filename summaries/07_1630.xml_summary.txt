The application before the Federal Magistrate sought judicial review of a decision of the Refugee Review Tribunal ("Tribunal") made on 26 February 1999 refusing to grant a protection visa to the applicant. 8 At the hearing counsel for the Minister advised the Court that the Minister would consent to orders granting the applicant leave to appeal from the Federal Magistrates' decision, setting aside the decision and remitting the matter to the Federal Magistrates' Court for a final hearing. The application was not filed until 23 April 2007, well outside the time provided for in s 477 of the Act .
