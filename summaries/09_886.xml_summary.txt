In para 83 of her statement of reasons the Minister accepted that the cancellation of Mr Rinka's visa would cause hardship to his two sons and said that she gave those considerations great weight in arriving at her decision. Mr Rinka submits that the concept of the best interests of the children involved consideration of their relationship with Mr Rinka. The Direction was divided into Part 1 ("Application of the Character Test") and Part 2 ("Exercising the Discretion"). The 2009 Amending Act contains transitional provisions. Paragraphs 2.13-2.16 elaborated on "the best interests of the child".
