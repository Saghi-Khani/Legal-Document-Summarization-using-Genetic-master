BRGAI of 2008 v Minister for Immigration and Citizenship [2009] FCA 1302 (13 November 2009) Failure to provide adequate opportunity to present arguments and give oral evidence". Section 424A(1)(a) requires that the Tribunal "give to the applicant...clear particulars of any information that the Tribunal considers would be the reason, or a part of the reason, for affirming the decision that is under review. The appellant claimed to have a well founded fear of persecution due to his practice of Falun Gong. The Tribunal fell into jurisdictional error in that it failed to disclose adverse findings.
