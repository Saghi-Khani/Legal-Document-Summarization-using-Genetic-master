Austral Holdings as head company became a former exempting entity at the joining time: the rule in item 1 of subs 709-165(2). The consolidation regime (Pt 3 -9, including Subdiv 709-B, of the ITAA 97) and new franking regime (Pt 3 -6, including Div 208, of the ITAA 97) commenced application. Hastie Group Limited v Commissioner of Taxation [2008] FCA 444 (7 April 2008) Ruling: No. The crucial factual event is that Austral Holdings was an exempting company (from 9 July 2003 to 6 February 2004) for a period of less than 12 months.
