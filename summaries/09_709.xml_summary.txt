The applicants seek orders that Mr Iannello give preliminary discovery of a number of categories of documents under O 15A r 3 and r 6 of the Federal Court Rules (the Rules). On 21 June 2008, Mr Murdoch sent Mr Iannello a letter. During the period of Mr Iannello's employment, BP Oil Refinery Pty Ltd (BP) was one of Total's major clients. Mr Iannello, the respondent, is a former employee of the second applicant, Total Corrosion Control Pty Ltd (Total). The letter also stated that their clients had previously acknowledged their obligations in respect of confidential information.
