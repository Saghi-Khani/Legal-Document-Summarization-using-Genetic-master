Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Commission v McGuire [2008] FCA 142 (6 March 2008) The Court appointed pro bono counsel and on 7 December 2007, Mr David McKenna as pro bono counsel advised Mr McGuire. His difficulty might well be understood. Section 27(1) provides that a judgment creditor may apply for a means inquiry to be held in respect of the judgment debtor. They failed. Compensation was declined. He pursued appeals.
