Parliament has laid down that the Federal Magistrates Court is precluded from exercising jurisdiction in a matter arising under any part of the Trade Practices Act apart from a matter under Pt V. This is so whether or not an action in respect of that mater has begun in the Federal Court. In the course of his argument against the transfer counsel for the University also pointed out the prosecutrix' claim "may in fact exceed the $200,000 jurisdictional limit". The Federal Magistrate refused to make the transfer order. Among other relief the prosecutrix claimed damages under s 82.
