Or in the alternative The Respondent be prohibited from proceeding with a hearing of the allegations particularized in their application dated 7/11/08 points 1, 2, 3, 7, 8, 9 at trial date 8-10 December. The fact that Ms George had brought proceedings in the Supreme Court of Queensland is, therefore, no reason to justify a stay of proceedings properly commenced by the trustee in bankruptcy in the Federal Magistrates Court. Thus, applications declaring for or against the title of the trustee to any property would be encompassed within the s 27 concept of jurisdiction in bankruptcy.
