His Honour there was dealing with whether it was appropriate to make an order for costs against the tribunal which had appeared as the active contestant in the proceedings, notwithstanding the observations of the Court in Hardiman (144 CLR at 35-36). 295 [2007] FCA 388) and made orders in the nature of a writ of certiorari quashing the findings of each of the committees.
