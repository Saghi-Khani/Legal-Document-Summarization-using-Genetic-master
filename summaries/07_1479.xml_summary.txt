Subsequently Mr Reid filed an affidavit in the Kokatha Native Title Claim in which he claimed to "hold all the native title rights or interests of the Kokatha people". The relevant authorisation is that by "all the persons ... who ... hold the common or group rights". I have referred to the KPC above, to its constitution and to the terms of its application form. Members acknowledge traditional laws and customs of the Kokatha people. It is in that group's favour that a determination, if successful, is made: ibid; see also Colbung v State of Western Australia [2003] FCA 774.
