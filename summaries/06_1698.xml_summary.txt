In my view, the Territory had reason to believe that ACOC would use the bark and wood from the timber in combination for the purpose of producing blue cypress oil. Consequently, the "supply" of the timber by the Territory to ACOC did not activate s 117(1) at all because the "use" referred to must be an infringing use.
