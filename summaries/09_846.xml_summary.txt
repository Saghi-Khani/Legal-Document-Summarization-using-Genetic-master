The Trinbay DOCA did specify circumstances in which that DOCA was to terminate. Neither concerned a payment of a final dividend to creditors. Accordingly, on 2 April 2003 the obligation to summon a meeting of creditors provided for in cl 3 of the prescribed provisions for the purpose of their passing a resolution under s 445C(b) of the Law was enlivened. Mr Gould accepted that it was not practicable to carry on that business for lack of funds available to Trinbay. Question (2) in ASIC's Notice of Contention was set out at [318] of the Earlier Reasons.
