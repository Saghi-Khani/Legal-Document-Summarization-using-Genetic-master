Mr Bertram has also foreshadowed that the liquidator of Firepower Operations may also seek to examine Mr Johnston under s 596A of the Corporations Act . Mr Johnston is a director of companies within the Firepower group, which are now in liquidation. The Court has power under s 1323(1) of the Corporations Act to make orders of the kind sought by ASIC. I am satisfied that there are persons, therefore, who are within the category of aggrieved persons.
