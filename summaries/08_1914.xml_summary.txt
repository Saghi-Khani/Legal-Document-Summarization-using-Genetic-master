Once the taxing master has made a final order with the respect to those costs, the order "may be enforced in the same manner as a judgment for the payment of money" (O 63 r 11(1)). That practice must be given affect for the purposes of the Bankruptcy Act . These costs were taxed and allowed at $123,587.41. The new rules did away with the taxing master's certificates and allocaturs. But the liability to pay costs depends upon the order made by the court that costs be paid by one party to another.
