113 Having regard to the whole of the evidence, I am satisfied that the products are goods of a kind ordinarily acquired for personal, domestic or household use or consumption within the meaning of s 74A(2)(a). Both uncoated reflective foil laminates and white-faced foil laminates are and were commonly left exposed to view in commercial and industrial buildings as the only form of roof or wall lining. Some reflective foil insulation products have an anti-glare coating and/or a decorative (usually white) facing. Hill J was less rigid about the characterisation of the chairs.
