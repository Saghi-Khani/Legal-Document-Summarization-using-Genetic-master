French J concluded that the transmission by a freelance journalist of a news article to a newspaper or other media outlet with whom he or she has a contract to supply such services was not a publication of matter " in connection " with the supply of such services. The proposed appeal challenges the construction, in certain respects, placed by the Court upon s 65A of the Trade Practices Act 1974 (Cth) (the Act): the so-called 'media safe harbour'. They said Mr Breen has also contravened s 52 of the Act. Bond v Barry [2007] FCA 2034 (18 December 2007)
