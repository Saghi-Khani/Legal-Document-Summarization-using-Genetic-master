That the respondent was and is entitled to recover the penalty referred to in the penalty notices from the applicant under section 222AOE of the tax act. Such returns were made pursuant to statutory requirement. Each of the five notices was sent by prepaid post to the applicant, addressed to her at Woolaston Road, Warrnambool Vic 3280. Section 222AOF is a facilitative provision. One method of service is the sending of notices by prepaid post to "an address" that "appears" from returns lodged by the relevant company with the ASC to be the director's place of residence or business.
