THE THUDGARI NATIVE TITLE APPLICATION  On 18 November 1997, the initial Thudgari native title determination application was lodged with the National Native Title Tribunal (Tribunal) pursuant to the Native Title Act 1993 (Cth) ( Native Title Act ). In assessing the appropriateness of a proposed determination the Court is not required to undertake an inquiry into the merits of the claim: Billy Patch and Others on behalf of the Birriliburu People v State of Western Australia [2008] FCA 944 at [13] per French J. Historical places, including the Mukurlu Massacre site, are also regarded as areas of cultural significance to the claimants.
