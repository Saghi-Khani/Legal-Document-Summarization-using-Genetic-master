It does not necessarily follow from the Tribunal's error that Australia Post is liable to pay Mr Mellor compensation. Did the Tribunal fail to consider whether Mr Mellor's work caused a permanent aggravation by increasing his background level of pain? It was usually related to physical activity. The Tribunal accepted that the employment caused temporary pain from Mr Mellor's constitutional thoracic and lumbar back conditions. That constituted the aggravation or exacerbation of the disease. "Disease" means an ailment or aggravation of an ailment ' that was contributed to in a material degree by the employee's employment '.
