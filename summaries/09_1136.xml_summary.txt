This parcel was transferred by the State of New South Wales to the applicant, on or about 31 March 2003, pursuant to the provisions of the Aboriginal Land Rights Act 1983 (NSW). In addition, both the first respondent (the Minister for Lands) and the second respondent (NTSCORP Limited) have filed notices indicating that pursuant to s 86G of the Native Title Act , they do not oppose orders in or consistent with the terms sought by the applicant, namely that no native title exists in relation to the land. Section 86G of the Native Title Act deals with unopposed applications.
