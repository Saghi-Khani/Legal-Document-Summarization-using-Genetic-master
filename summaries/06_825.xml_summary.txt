Maxwell-Smith v S & E Hall Pty Limited, in the matterof Maxwell-Smith [2006] FCA 825 (3 July 2006) Introduction   1 On 30 November 1999, Mr and Mrs Maxwell-Smith made an application for special leave to appeal to the High Court from a judgment and order of the New South Wales Court of Appeal. I understand this to be a claim that the issue of the bankruptcy notice is an abuse of process. The only named creditor was S & E Hall. He ordered Mr and Mrs Maxwell-Smith to pay S & E Hall's costs on an indemnity basis.
