Tasman Group Services Pty Ltd v Commissioner of Taxation of the Commonwealth of Australia (No 2) [2008] FCA 218 (3 March 2008) The appeal in each application be allowed. The Applicant pay the Respondent's costs in the Notice of Motion filed on behalf of the Applicant on 10 February 2006.
