Alleged breach of s 425 of the Act  18 Section 425(1) of the Act provides as follows:  The Tribunal must invite the applicant to appear before the Tribunal to give evidence and present arguments relating to the issues arising in relation to the decision under review. He added nothing more. Turner FM also noted that the appellant had appeared and given evidence at the Tribunal hearing and accordingly rejected the appellant's claim. Since no jurisdictional error is established, the Court dismisses this ground of appeal. SZGQZ v Minister for Immigration and Citizenship [2007] FCA 1091 (18 October 2007)
