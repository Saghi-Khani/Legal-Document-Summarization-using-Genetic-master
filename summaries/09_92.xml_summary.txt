The first of the matters 'of concern' referred to by the Tribunal was that after the Turkish divorce on 3 February 2003, (but well before the appellant married the sponsor on 28 January 2005), the appellant had made two visa applications in which he said he was "married. For the above reasons, I therefore consider that in reaching the decision in the present case the Tribunal member has taken into account a number of irrelevant considerations, and that this constitutes jurisdictional error. The lack of a 'business case' does not reasonably cast doubt on the relationship which began later.
