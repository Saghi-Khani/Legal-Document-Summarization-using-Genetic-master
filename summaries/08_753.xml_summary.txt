The plaintiff proposes to bring a proceeding to have the consent orders made by the Family Court on 30 August 2006 set aside. According to Ms Asciak, she asked the defendant companies' bookkeeper and external accountants to provide PPB Forensics with any information it requested. Insofar as the plaintiff needs to rely on the tax liability representations to make good his case he is not able to do so because of the provisions of s 131(1) of the Evidence Act . Both the plaintiff and Ms Asciak were involved in the business. JLC Valuers supplied valuations on 3 June 2006.
