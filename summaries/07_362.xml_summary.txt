The Federal Magistrate's reasons  7 Federal Magistrate McInnis dismissed the amended application ( MZXFN v Minister for Immigration & Multicultural Affairs [2006] FMCA 1274). Documents from doctors in Sialkot, dated 10 July 2004 and 20 August 2004, were also provided ('the medical reports'). Nor did the Tribunal accept that the appellant left Pakistan illegally. The Tribunal did not make a finding of forgery or otherwise of the documents but considered the documents on their face to see whether they did corroborate the appellant's claims. Further particulars are not given. Associate:Dated: 15 March 2007 The Appellant appeared in person.
