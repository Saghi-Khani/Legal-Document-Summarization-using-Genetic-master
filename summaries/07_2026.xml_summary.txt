The Act does not take away or abrogate legal professional privilege. The functions of the Board include authorising the Commission to investigate matters relating to federally relevant criminal activity and determining whether such an investigation is a special investigation . The Examiner who decided to issue the notices intended not to give to the applicants any prior opportunity to assert any claim for legal professional privilege over any documents that were the subject of notices issued to persons or entities other than the applicants. The Examiner must also record in writing the reasons for the issue of the summons.
