A general-purpose road vehicle is defined by s 3(2) of the STEC Act to mean 'a road vehicle of a kind ordinarily used for the transport of persons or the transport or delivery of goods or other property'. Reliance was placed by Lindsay upon the definition of manufacture-related activity appearing in Item 18(5), and which has been earlier extracted therefrom. That submission was soundly conceived. An exemption Item is defined also in s 3 to mean 'an Item or subitem in Schedule 1'.
