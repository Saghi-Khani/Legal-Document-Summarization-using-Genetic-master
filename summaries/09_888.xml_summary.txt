The second issue is whether the Tribunal had completed the performance of its statutory function when the appellant handed to the Tribunal officer who was about to hand down the Tribunal's decision an additional document, which he said was relevant to his case. His Honour held that all of the claims referred to in the appellant's application to the Federal Magistrates Court had been dealt with. One was the appellant's evidence that the microchip on his Malaysian identity card recorded him as a Muslim.
