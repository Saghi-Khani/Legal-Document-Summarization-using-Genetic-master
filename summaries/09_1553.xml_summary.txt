DEFENDANTS' CRITICISMS OF THE AMENDED PLEADING  Paragraph 3  In par 3 of the amended pleading, the plaintiffs seek to list in a comprehensive fashion but in summary form the matters of complaint which they wish to agitate concerning the conduct of Premier's affairs. During the course of argument, Senior Counsel for the second to fourth defendants, conceded that Russell and Helen received directors' fees from Perfection over several years and that they did not account to Premier for any part of those fees. On 10 November 2009, the plaintiffs filed Interlocutory Process. Russell and Victor Halfpenny were brothers.
