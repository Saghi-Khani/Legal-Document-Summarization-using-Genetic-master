The appellant filed a notice of appeal in this Court appealing from the judgment of the Federal Magistrates Court on 15 May 2007. MZXPC v Minister for Immigration and Citizenship [2007] FCA 1075 (23 July 2007) The appellant has not sought leave, notwithstanding that the defect is apparent from the first respondent's notice of objection to competency. The Federal Magistrate dismissed an application for review of a decision of the Refugee Review Tribunal ('the Tribunal') handed down on 2 April 2004 affirming a decision of a delegate of the first respondent not to grant the appellant a protection visa.
