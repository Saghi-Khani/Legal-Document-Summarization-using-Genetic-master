However, the applicant companies are Perth-based. Indeed, as the applicants suggest, a transfer may require both sides to engage Sydney-based representatives. By motion dated 31 March 2009, the respondent applied, pursuant to s 48 of the Federal Court of Australia Act 1976 (Cth) and O 10 r 1(2)(f) of the Rules, for an order transferring the proceedings to the Court's New South Wales District Registry at Sydney. It is reasonably trite to say that in a national court like this, parties can indeed participate in proceedings in one registry from other parts of the country.
