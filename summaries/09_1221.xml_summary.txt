Motor Trades Association of Australia Superannuation Fund Pty Ltd v Rickus (No 5) [2009] FCA 1221 (28 October 2009) Knowledge is not agreement. When addressing the question as to whether there was a valid retainer between Mr Rickus and his legal advisers, the written submissions went well beyond a reference to the terms of the 16 November 2006 letter. On 18 June 2009 Deputy District Registrar Cujes made orders that:   1. Passing references to " litigation " cannot transform this letter into an agreement as to the terms upon which the solicitors would conduct litigation on Mr Rickus's behalf.
