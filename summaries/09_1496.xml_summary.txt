Clark v Commissioner of Taxation (No. 2) [2009] FCA 1496 (14 December 2009) The appropriate order is an order that the respondent pay the costs of each applicant of and incidental to the application. Section 14ZZP of the Taxation Administration Act 1953 (Cth) provides that the Court may make such order in relation to the appellable objection decision as it thinks fit including an order varying the decision. The Commissioner of Taxation is directed to issue an Amended Assessment taking account of the reasons for judgment published on 30 November 2009. Short submissions were received as to those matters.
