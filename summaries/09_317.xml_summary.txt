This application was before the Court on 2 March 2009 for directions. Dr Donaghue, counsel for the second and third respondents, contended that the declaration which is sought could not be made for two reasons. Furthermore, the motion of 26 March 2009 was short served, and at least the first respondent was not in a position to obtain instructions about the contents of the affidavit in support. Ms Desai responded by asking for a declaration of genocide. Secondly, the Court made directions requiring the applicants to define with precision the legal basis upon which they brought their claim.
