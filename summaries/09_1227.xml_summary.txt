However s 55B provides that entitlement to practise as a barrister or solicitor, or both, in the Supreme Court of a State or Territory confers the like entitlement to practise in any federal court. Under O 62, r 8(1) of the FCR, the taxing officer can only allow and certify costs and fees which are in fact payable to legal practitioners of the defined type, that is, whose names appear in the Register of Practitioners kept by the High Court under the Judiciary Act . The costs of the proceeding in respect of the determination of these costs is reserved.
