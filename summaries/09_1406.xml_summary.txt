Regulation 856.222 requires the Minister to be satisfied that the appointment in paragraph 856.213 (a) will provide the employment referred to in the relevant employer nomination. On 17 February 2005 Revetec Limited lodged an application for approval of a nominated position pursuant to reg 5.19 of the Migration Regulations 1994 (Cth) (the Regulations). The Federal Magistrate upheld the Tribunal's decision. Revetec Limited nominated the appellant as an employee as sales and marketing manager. Revetec Holdings Limited was incorporated on 4 August 2005 to acquire the business assets of Revetec Limited.
