From January 2002 many other airlines imposed fuel surcharges in accordance with the Lufthansa Methodology. Moreover, the Fuel Surcharge Understanding saved each airline the very cost of itself calculating the responses it should make from time to time to fuel price increases. The conduct substantially reduced competition for the carriage of air cargo. As a result of those discussions Qantas and the Commission have reached agreement as to the appropriate penalty to be suggested to the Court.
