In an attached notice of general meeting for 12 February 2007, the special business was listed as (1) the passage of an ordinary resolution to set the number of directors of the company at five pursuant to cl 13.1 of Reefton's Constitution; (2) the re-election of Mr Thompson; and (3) the consideration of the election of the remaining seven candidates, including the four candidates referred to in the shareholders' notice of meeting. An election of Directors shall take place each year. Reefton submits that the defendants' notice does not indicate any intention to comply with that requirement.
