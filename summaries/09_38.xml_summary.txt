Georges, In the Matter of Midas Australia Pty Ltd (Administrators Appointed) [2009] FCA 38 (29 January 2009) The availability of a surplus from the sale of the Midas Australia business would also impact on any proposed deed of company arrangement. Further, Mr Georges has deposed that: Pursuant to s 439A(1) of the Corporations Act , the administrator of a company under administration must convene a meeting of creditors within the convening period as fixed by s 439A(5) , unless extended by the Court under s 439A(6). At this meeting, the creditors established a committee of creditors.
