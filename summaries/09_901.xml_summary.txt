Several landlords, among them the defendants, have served notices under s 568(8) of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) giving the liquidators of TL and TSL 28 days within which to disclaim the leases. Each investor was granted a sub-lease of part of the land upon which his scheme conducted operations. Strict duties are imposed on a responsible entity under s 601FC. What follows are the reasons. Prior to its collapse the Timbercorp group promoted a range of managed investment schemes that involved forestry and horticultural operations. The liquidator is fiduciary. That rent was not paid.
