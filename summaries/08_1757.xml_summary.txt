The Federal Magistrate found that respondent's employment had been terminated in circumstances proscribed by s 659(2)(a) of the WR Act and imposed a pecuniary penalty on the applicants: Barhoum v All Districts Coating Pty Ltd & Ors [2008] FMCA 172 (22 February 2008) . On the evidence before the Court, that at least since 2004, his business operated using the two companies interchangeably for business purposes. An order staying the orders of the learned Federal Magistrate pending determination of this appeal. These orders were made against the first, second and third applicants. This judgment concerns that application.
