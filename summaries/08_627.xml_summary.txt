pSivida Limited v New pSivida, Inc; in the matter of pSivida Limited [2008] FCA 627 (1 May 2008) 15 I am satisfied that I should make orders in accordance with the short minutes of order which I have now signed and dated and placed with the Court papers. However, the liabilities comprising the options will be transferred to New pSivida and will become liabilities of that company. An ADS is a security that is tradable on NASDAQ and the Frankfurt Stock Exchange. If the scheme is approved, pSivida shareholders will receive one CDI for every 40 ordinary shares in pSivida.
