Hillig as Administrator of Worimi Local Aboriginal Land Council v NSW Native Title Services Ltd [2006] FCA 1184 (1 September 2006) Mr Hillig, in his capacity as Administrator of the Land Council, brings an application pursuant to s 61(1) in Part 3 of the Act seeking a determination that native title does not exist in relation to the land. Mr Hillig submits that the application is unopposed and that the Court may make the order sought without holding a hearing. There is no evidence before the Court of any native title rights or interests in the land.
