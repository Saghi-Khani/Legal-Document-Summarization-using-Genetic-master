In this case, there is an unusual feature because in 2008 proceedings were commenced in Azerbaijan claiming compensation against the respondent in respect of the alleged breaches of duty and misappropriation referred to in the statement of claim. Section 5(4) states that:  Subject to subsection (8), each provision of this Act also applies, according to its tenor, in relation to acts and omissions outside this jurisdiction. There is also a discretion in the Court which may be exercised to refuse leave to serve the proceeding out of the jurisdiction. The applicant is a company incorporated in Australia.
