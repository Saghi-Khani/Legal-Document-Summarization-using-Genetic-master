Clauses 856.21 and 856.22 in Schedule 2 to the Regulations specify the criteria that must be satisfied for the grant of a subclass 856 visa. That is to say, the Tribunal considered that the question before it was whether the Visa Applicant, at the time of her application, was a highly skilled person in relation to the approved appointment of sales and marketing manager in the business of the Employer. Her Honour interpreted r 5.19(3)(a) to require the work experience to be equivalent to at least three years of formal training.
