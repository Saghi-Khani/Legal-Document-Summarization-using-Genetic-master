However, the appellant was on notice, having regard to the delegate's decision, that his claim, based on a risk of persecution because of what he had done in his position with the hotel, might not be accepted by the Tribunal. The Tribunal misinterpreted the word, "systematic" in s 91R(1)(c) of the Migration Act in connection with the harm that Christians in Nepal may face from Hindu fundamentalists. On 21 November 2006 the appellants lodged an application for protection visas with the Department of Immigration and Citizenship. The Federal Magistrate did not err in reaching the same conclusion.
