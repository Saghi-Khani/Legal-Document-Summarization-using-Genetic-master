DOES REFLECTED LIGHT CONTRIBUTE SUBSTANTIALLY TO THE FLAME EFFECT IN THE RESPONDENTS' HEATERS  Putting to one side the light source itself and the viewing screen, in the respondents' heaters, the simulated flame effect means comprises at least the rotating cylinder and the flame shaped cut out plate. Garth Living Pty Ltd has imported and sold the MS-5 heater. Claim 1 is the critical Claim. The ribbons can also be coated with material to improve their reflectivity. Professor Nugent conducted an experiment in order to assess the contribution that diffuse reflection made to the flame effect in the respondents' heaters.
