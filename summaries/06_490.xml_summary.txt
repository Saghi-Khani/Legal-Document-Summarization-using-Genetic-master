Wong v Minister for Immigration & Multicultural & Indigenous Affairs [2006] FCA 490 (5 May 2006) The interlocutory proceedings for determination of the viability of the respondents' claim to public interest immunity advanced pursuant to the Attorney General's certificate bearing date 10 April 2005 be referred to the Full Court for hearing and determination pursuant to s 25(6) of the Federal Court of Australia Act 1976 . Both parties were represented by counsel on the present application. I was also referred in that regard to s 36A(1) and (2) of the AAT Act.
