The Federal Magistrate dismissed as incompetent an application for judicial review of a decision of the Refugee Review Tribunal handed down on 26 May 2005. 1 On 15 November 2006 a notice of motion was filed in this Court seeking to reopen a judgment of Lander J of 6 November 2006: see SZISF v Minister for Immigration and Multicultural Affairs [2006] FCA 1462. His Honour gave judgment in the absence of the applicant who did not appear at the hearing. The order made by Lander J was entered on 8 November 2006. The 84 days expired on 23 February 2006 .
