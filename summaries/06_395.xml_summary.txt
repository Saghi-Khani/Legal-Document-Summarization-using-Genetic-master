'      25 Temporary payment at special rate   (1) Where the Commission is satisfied that:      (a) a veteran is temporarily incapacitated from war-caused injury or war-caused disease, or both; and   (b) if the veteran were so incapacitated permanently, the veteran would be a veteran to whom section 24 applies;       the Commission shall determine the period during which, in its opinion, that incapacity is likely to continue and this section applies to the veteran in respect of that period. Use of the word "never" supports the Deputy President's approach. The applicant receives a pension pursuant to the Act.
