Suppose an airport lessee specified in a draft master plan under s 71(2)(a) a development objective to construct a new runway in the five year period which the draft plan was to cover. Issue 1: Must Airservices endorse the assumptions in an ANEF? An ultimate capacity ANEF is one which assesses the maximum number of aircraft movements of which the relevant airport is capable, and then makes a noise exposure forecast of land in the vicinity of the airport in respect of that number. Airservices' functions are contained in s 8 of the Air Services Act .
