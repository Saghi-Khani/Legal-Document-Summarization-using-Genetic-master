APPLICATION FOR ORDER UNDER S 661A(3) OF THE CA  Part 6A.1 of the CA deals with compulsory acquisitions and buy-outs following a takeover bid. Mr Grant Michael Button, Director and Joint Company Secretary of Sylvania, explained in his affidavit made 13 August 2009 in support of the originating process and read in the proceeding, why the plaintiff now seeks an order extending the period for acceptance of the GAU offer, or, in the alternative, obtaining the approval of the Court to compulsorily acquire the remaining securities. Sylvania's relevant interest in GAU increased to 89.82%.
