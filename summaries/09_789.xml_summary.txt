Regulation 4(1) of the Native Title (Prescribed Bodies Corporate) Regulations 1999 (Cth) ("the Native Title Regulations") provides that an Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander corporation is prescribed if it is registered for the purposes of being the subject of a s 57 determination. Through their continued acknowledgement and observance of these normative laws and customs, the Wik and Wik Way Peoples have, since sovereignty, maintained a connection with the Determination Area. The agreement was filed in the Court on 6 July 2009. They must be rights and interests in relation to land or waters.
