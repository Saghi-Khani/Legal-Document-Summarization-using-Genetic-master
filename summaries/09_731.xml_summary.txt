Clause 10 provides 24 April 2008 (the date of appointment of the administrators) to be the day on which the creditors' claims must have arisen. For the reasons already given, an order under s 444F does not reinstate the Lease. BCC also contends that the Court cannot make an order under s 444F to restrain an owner or lessor from taking possession or recovering property after the DOCA has been performed. At that stage the orders made under s 444F would be discharged by the further breach. I refused the application. The principal relief sought was under the Corporations Act .
