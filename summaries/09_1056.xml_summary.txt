The appellants claim to be Bangladeshi homosexuals, who would face a real risk of persecution should they be returned to Bangladesh. The Tribunal also found that the appellants' witness lacked credibility and did not accept his evidence. I am satisfied that the decision by the Tribunal on the third occasion the matter was before it was not made in good faith. This appeal by the appellants to this Court will be allowed.
