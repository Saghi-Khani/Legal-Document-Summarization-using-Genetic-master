In my view the finding of the Tribunal with respect to the view the Iranian authorities would take of his United States navy service, and accordingly the unlikelihood of the appellant being subject to persecution in Iran, was not open on the evidence before it. The decision of the Refugee Review Tribunal be quashed. The state of satisfaction that there was no well founded fear of persecution was not formed reasonably on the material before the Tribunal: Applicant M164/2002 v Minister for Immigration and Multicultural and Indigenous Affairs [2006] FCAFC 16.
