Australian Prudential Regulation Authority v Siminton (No 12) [2008] FCA 101 (21 February 2008) 1 By order made herein dated 7 November 2007 I appointed Mr Colin Nicol as receiver to deal with certain funds which had been held in the names of the respondent, the Terra Nova Cache and the Principality of Camside. The order would also require him to identify any claims of privilege he might wish to make in respect of any of these documents. The receiver had obtained the additional information which he had sought from APRA from third parties.
