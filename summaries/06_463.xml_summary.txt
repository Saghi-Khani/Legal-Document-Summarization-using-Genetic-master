Safe Effects Technologies Ltd v London Partners Australia Pty Ltd [2006] FCA 463 (12 April 2006) 3 Section 444D(1) of the Act provides that a deed of company arrangement binds all creditors of the company so far as concerns claims arising before the date of commencement of the administration or a prior date fixed by the deed. Therefore, leave will be granted to SETL to proceed with the proceeding brought against London Partners. The costs of the application will be reserved.
