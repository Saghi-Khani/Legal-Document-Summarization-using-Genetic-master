1 By application dated 3 August 2007, the applicant ('Westpac') applies to this Court for leave pursuant to s 58(3)(b) of the Bankruptcy Act 1966 (Cth) ('the Act') to proceed against the respondent ('Mr Ollis') up to judgment in Supreme Court proceedings No. 8 On 21 November 2006 Westpac obtained orders in the Supreme Court of New South Wales which restrained Mr Ollis from dealing with property and assets claimed by Westpac. Section 82 of the Act provides the nature of debts provable in bankruptcy. The proceedings were brought by Westpac against Mr Ollis and other defendants.
