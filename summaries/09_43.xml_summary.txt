Phisci Pty Ltd v Green Frog Nominees Pty Ltd (in liq) (No 3) [2009] FCA 43 (4 February 2009) The second defendant, Opes Prime Stockbroking Pty Ltd (receiver and manager appointed) (in liquidation) (OPS), also claims to be the beneficial owner. On 15 December 2008 I granted Phisci leave to proceed with its action against OPS for purposes of determining the ownership of the shares and issues of liability: leave was required by reason of s 500(2) of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth). Phisci pleads several causes of action to found its claim for the RBNZ shares.
